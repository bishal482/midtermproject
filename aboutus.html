About Neta

Neta was founded in 1999 with a vision to revolutionize the transportation industry through innovative electric vehicles and sustainable energy solutions. Over the past two decades, Neta has made a remarkable impact on the world, leading the charge towards a greener and more sustainable future.

Since its inception, Neta has been dedicated to pioneering technologies that enhance mobility while reducing the environmental footprint. Our commitment to sustainability and innovation has positioned us as a global leader in electric vehicles, autonomous driving technology, and artificial intelligence.

Through our three key sub-brands—MOTO, LEDA, and Sunglung—we have successfully expanded our offerings to address various sectors of the tech and automotive industries:

  • MOTO: Pioneering the online job market, MOTO connects millions with remote work opportunities, empowering freelancers and job seekers globally.
  • LEDA: Renowned for its state-of-the-art moto-engines, LEDA provides sustainable and high-performance power solutions for electric and hybrid vehicles.
  • Sunglung: A leading gaming screen manufacturer, Sunglung creates cutting-edge displays that deliver immersive experiences for gamers and professionals alike.

As a testament to our commitment to excellence, Neta has garnered numerous awards for innovation and sustainability, further solidifying our position as a pioneer in the tech industry. We are proud of our history and excited about our future as we continue to innovate and inspire.

Join us on our journey as we drive the future of technology forward, creating a better, more sustainable world for generations to come.
